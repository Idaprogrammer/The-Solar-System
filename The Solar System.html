The Solar System


  • Our solar system consists of 8 planets that orbit around the sun
  • In order from closest to farthest from the sun the planets go as follows: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.
  • After Neptune comes Pluto, which is no longer considered a planet but a dwarf planet instead.
  • Our solar system consists of more than just planets. Asteroids, comets, stars, moons and many other things make up the mystical solar system we call home.
